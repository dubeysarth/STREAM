2. Add or wire a monthly lumped outlet baseline on the same US monthly split as the distributed code.
3. Evaluate the distributed GConvLSTM on that same US monthly split with outlet and node-level summaries.
4. Add one topology-off control on the same split and inputs.
5. Quantify the India zero-shot failure-patch story with reservoir and intermittency summaries.

The goal is not to build a benchmark zoo. The goal is to close the most visible reviewer gaps with the smallest coherent experiment set.
